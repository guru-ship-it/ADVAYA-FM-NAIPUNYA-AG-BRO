# Data Deletion Request (Advaya FM NaipuNya)

## Your Right to Delete Data

Under the **DPDP Act 2023** and **Google Play Data Safety Policies**, you have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and associated data.

## How to Request Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to our Data Protection Officer (DPO):
**Email**: <privacy@advaya-elite.com>
**Subject**: "DELETE MY DATA - [Your Employee ID]"

## What Happens Next?

1. **Verification**: We will verify your identity via your registered mobile number using an OTP.
2. **Compliance Check**: We will check if any data must be retained for legal/MNC audits (e.g., proof of safety training).
3. **Deletion**: All non-essential data (photos, preferences) is wiped within **30 days**.
4. **Confirmation**: You will receive a final confirmation email.

*Note: Deletion of your profile may result in loss of "Vajra" Verified Status and site access.*
