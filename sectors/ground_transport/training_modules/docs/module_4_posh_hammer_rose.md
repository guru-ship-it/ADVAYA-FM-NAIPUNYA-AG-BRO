# Module 4: POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment)

## "Hammer & Rose"

**Persona**: Dual (Didi - Supportive / Vajra - Strict) | **Duration**: 20 Mins

### Overview

A critical, dual-track module. "Plan Rose" empowers support staff, while "Plan Hammer" educates drivers on boundaries and consequences.

### Key Syllabus

1. **Boundaries**: Physical and verbal limits. No "friendly" touching or personal questions.
2. **Consent**: Understanding that "No" means "No", and silence does not mean "Yes".
3. **Plan Rose (For Women)**: How to report safely via the app (Zero-Reprisal).
4. **Plan Hammer (For Offenders)**: Immediate termination, blacklisting, and legal action.
5. **The "Vajra Eye"**: Reminding that the system is always watching for safety deviations.

### Didi's Message

*"Safety is not just on the road. It is in how we treat each other. Use your strength to protect, never to harm."*
