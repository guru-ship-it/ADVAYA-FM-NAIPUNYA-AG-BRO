# Module 6: Intermediate Driving

## "City Mastery"

**Persona**: Vajra (Tactical) | **Duration**: 15 Mins

### Overview

Navigating the chaotic urban environment (Bangalore/Hyderabad traffic) with grace and efficiency.

### Key Syllabus

1. **Lane Discipline**: Why weaving saves seconds but risks lives.
2. **Junction Logic**: Right of way, roundabouts, and signal discipline.
3. **Aggressive Traffic**: How to remain calm when others are not. "Zen Driving".
4. **Passenger Comfort**: Smooth braking/acceleration so the CEO in the back doesn't spill coffee.

### Vajra's Command

*"Chaos is the test. Calm is the answer. Drive smooth, arrive safe."*
