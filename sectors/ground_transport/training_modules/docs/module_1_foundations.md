# Module 1: Foundations

## "The Spirit of Service"

**Persona**: Didi (Supportive) | **Duration**: 12 Mins

### Overview

The foundational module introducing the driver to the "Elite" ecosystem and the specific culture of the client (Broadcom).

### Key Syllabus

1. **Who is a Pilot?**: Shifting the mindset from "Driver" to "Corporate Pilot".
2. **The Client (Broadcom)**: Understanding the prestige and expectations of serving top-tier executives.
3. **The Promise**: "Safe. Punctual. Silent."
4. **Zero-Reprisal Policy (POSH)**: Introduction to the safety net.

### Didi's Message

*"Welcome to the Elite family. You are not just turning a wheel; you are carrying the minds that build the future. Your dignity is our priority, and your service is their safety."*
