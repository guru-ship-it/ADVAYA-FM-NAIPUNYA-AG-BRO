# Module 7: Advanced Driving

## "Tactical Safety"

**Persona**: Vajra (Expert) | **Duration**: 20 Mins

### Overview

Handling adverse conditions and high-risk scenarios. Night driving and Monsoon tactics.

### Key Syllabus

1. **Night Ops**: Glare management, fatigue recognition, and visibility.
2. **Monsoon Tactics**: Hydroplaning, braking distances in wet, flooded roads.
3. **Tyre Blowout**: The "Do Not Brake" rule. Stabilization protocol.
4. **Breakdown Protocol**: Securing the VIP, safe positioning, and calling for backup.

### Vajra's Command

*"The road is an enemy that changes its face. Master the dark and the rain."*
